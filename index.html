Assemble PC (lab)

REFLECTION:In our first lab session, our team of five learned the fascinating world of PC building. The day opens with excitement while disassembling the PC, performing steps methodically such as removing the case, disconnecting the cable, removing the power supply, RAM, hard disk drive, and finally, the fan. Each component reveals its secret, turning assignments into hands-on learning experiences that provide a satisfying sense of accomplishment. The challenge extended to rebuilding the PC within time constraints, where our teamwork and new knowledge proved successful on the first attempt. For someone familiar with DIY PCs, the process is easy, but witnessing the enthusiasm of beginners adds a fresh and valuable perspective. Post-lab quizzes add a touch of scoring to our hands-on adventure, creating a day full of fun and immersive experiences in PC assembly and disassembly.
